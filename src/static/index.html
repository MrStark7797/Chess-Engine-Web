Slice Chess

Slice Chess is a free, open source, chess learning and analysis program
which is backed by a high rating chess Engine,
in order to help improve users understanding and knowledge about the game of chess.

Why Chess?

Over 2023 chess experienced an explosive growth
One of the most popular websites, chess.com, had 1,057,320,754 games played in February alone
due to many factors including:
Hanns vs Magnus: pro chess cheating scandal,
Lionel Messi vs Cristiano Ronaldo Chess game posted on twitter.
Causing Lots of new young players to leak onto the scene.
